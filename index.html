    Our school canteen is very well equipped. One can buy almost every kind of snack there, like samosas, chips, sandwiches, sweet, cakes etc. ...   • Online Atendence Management System

    Our project is online attendance management system and the objectives are: 1. Reduce admin work by integrating the details of the students of all the three departments into a single database 2. Manual work for retrieval on attendance becomes less as the work becomes digital
